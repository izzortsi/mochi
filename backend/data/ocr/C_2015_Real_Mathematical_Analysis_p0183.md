A **step function** is a finite sum of constants times characteristic functions of intervals and is therefore Riemann integrable. A step function is a special type of **piecewise continuous** function, i.e., a function that is continuous except at finitely many points. See Figure 72. Bounded piecewise continuous functions are Riemann integrable. See Corollary 25 below.
